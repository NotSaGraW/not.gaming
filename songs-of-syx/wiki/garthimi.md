# Garthimi

> Garthimi love death and destruction while caring little for the finer things in life.
> High reproductive rate makes them perfect cannon fodder, but given the right training
> and equipment, they can become quite proficient. Their ability to raise cavern
> Balticrawlers gives ample food to feed their hive. They require humidifiers to reach an
> ideal environment.
>
> Garthimi were created by the fallen god Aminion in the first war of the gods. They
> reproduce quickly and their exoskeleton is quite resistant to all kinds of weather,
> while also providing a natural battle boost. Despite their violent nature, they are very
> lawful and place great importance on the well-being of their hive. They are good miners
> and the only species that can make use of Balticrawlers. They lack dextrous hands,
> limiting most industry, but make excellent stonecutters. Ambush predators at heart, they
> require training to be useful in battle, but well-trained Garthimi make excellent infantry.

Playable. Challenge: Easy.
