# Law & Order (v71 rework)

Completely redesigned in v71. Core trade: **happiness for loyalty**. A true utopia (100% happiness → near-zero crime) needs almost no law; you can play the whole game without touching it. Otherwise: set a punishment per crime; each crime+punishment combination shifts happiness and loyalty differently. Punishing severe crimes (murder) is nearly free — citizens approve; punishing trivial "crimes" (criticism) only pays once law infrastructure is deep. Each punished crime accumulates "tyranny".

**All law effects are multiplied by infrastructure:** guard count, guard post coverage, execution site coverage, secret police. Deep investment gives *exponential* returns → dystopian high-loyalty playstyle. Loyalty is often higher than happiness for a lawful ruler.

Guards (v71): active-duty soldiers you assign from trained troops; they patrol streets and tend posts; training and equipment improve law enforcement. Two guard room types radiate law-improving auras. Gallows are placeable items worked by guards, radiating a "dread" aura that boosts loyalty. Secret police (repurposed old slaver building) deters crime at some happiness/loyalty cost. Death monuments (gargoyles, skull mounds, heads on spikes) support the tyranny aesthetic/economy — heavy law lets you skip expensive services.

Dev caveat (devlog): new mechanics are "usually pretty crappy until refined in later versions" — expect balance patches.
