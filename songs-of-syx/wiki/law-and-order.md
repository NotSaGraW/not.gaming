# Law & Order (v71 rework)

Completely redesigned in v71. Core trade: **happiness for loyalty**. A true utopia (100%
happiness → near-zero crime) needs almost no law; you can play the whole game without
touching it. Otherwise you set a punishment per crime, and each crime+punishment
combination shifts happiness and loyalty differently. Punishing severe crimes (murder) is
nearly free — citizens approve; punishing trivial "crimes" (criticism) only pays once law
infrastructure is deep. Every punished crime accumulates "tyranny". Loyalty is often higher
than happiness for a lawful ruler.

**All law effects are multiplied by infrastructure** — deep investment gives *exponential*
returns (the dystopian high-loyalty playstyle):

| Tool | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Guards | Active-duty soldiers assigned from trained troops; patrol streets and tend posts; training and equipment improve enforcement. |
| Guard rooms | Two types, each radiating a law-improving aura. |
| Gallows | Placeable items worked by guards; radiate a "dread" aura that boosts loyalty. |
| Secret police | Repurposed old slaver building; deters crime at some happiness/loyalty cost. |
| Death monuments | Gargoyles, skull mounds, heads on spikes; heavy law lets you skip expensive services. |

Dev caveat (devlog): new mechanics are "usually pretty crappy until refined in later
versions" — expect balance patches.
