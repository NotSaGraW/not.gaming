# Cantor

> Cantors are mythical giants. The few of them that remain are primarily found in the
> north. They excel at battle, but are difficult to obtain and please.
>
> Cantors were the protectors of the Istari themselves. They fought alongside them from
> the first war to the final War of the Gods. The few that survived retreated north.
> Having one in your city is a privilege and they excel at warring and are very religious.
> Don't be thoughtless with their lives, as there are not many of them left. They have a
> great distaste for humans.

Non-playable (haven-gated). No climate/terrain profile; immigration ×0.
