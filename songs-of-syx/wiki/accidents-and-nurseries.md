# Accidents & the global nursery

v71: accident rates reduced and **disabled below 150 employees**, scaling up exponentially with larger populations; tech mitigates. Old nurseries replaced by a **global nursery system**: children spend days there, greatly reducing accidents and child mortality, and providing fulfilment to both slaves and citizens.

Community assessment (2026-07-06, #gameplay-q-and-a, two regulars): nurseries don't reduce parenting time — they corral kids to prevent accidents; "accident rates in v71 are way too low to bother"; nurseries cost a lot of space/labor for what they do, but are "a zero input satisfaction booster". *(confidence of this paragraph: community-claim — flag: verify accident scaling at high pop before dismissing nurseries.)*
